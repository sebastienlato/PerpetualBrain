# ShadowSpire Lessons Learned

- Do not wire generated assets until screenshot-approved.
- Do not accept placeholder visuals after a polish phase starts.
- Keep review routes for every major scene.
- Every phase must include build, typecheck, screenshot, and QA notes.
- Short Codex tasks work better when the exact level, scene, and acceptance criteria are named.
- Visual effects should be tuned in the running scene, not judged only from isolated asset previews.
