# ShadowSpire Design Rules

## Gameplay Readability
- The player silhouette must be recognizable at 1280x720 and 390x844.
- Hazards use red-orange only when they cause damage.
- Player spells use cyan, violet, or gold depending on spell family.
- Background particles should never cross the player at full opacity.

## UI Direction
- HUD should be compact, anchored, and legible over dark environments.
- Avoid large decorative cards during gameplay.
- Use icons first, short labels second.
- Every debug route should show the actual scene, not a static approximation.

## Asset Rules
- Do not wire generated assets until screenshot-approved.
- Keep sprite anchors consistent across animation strips.
- Export transparent PNG sheets with a one-pixel padding gutter.
